Problem Statement

2. Problem Statement

In today’s modern lifestyle, people—especially students—are becoming less physically active due to increased use of mobile phones, computers, and television. This lack of physical activity leads to health problems such as obesity, stress, and low stamina. Many students also do not understand the importance of sports in their daily life. Schools and colleges sometimes do not give enough attention to sports activities, resulting in reduced participation.
